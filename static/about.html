About BNW

Brave New World (BNW) is a Final Fantasy VI romhack by BTB and Synchysi that has been in active development for over ten years and is currently maintained and updated by a host of people associated with the ngplus.net community.

The information contained on this page comes from an 8-page PDF file that is packaged with the romhack that most people call "the printme." A lot of work must have gone into containing this great deal of information into a readable, printable format, and I recommend that you print it out yourself to reference as you play the mod.

If you're like me, there is going to come a point where you wish that you could search the document quickly for exactly what you're looking for. That will bring you to this page, "the searchme."
